Who am I?

I'm Morteza, and I'm a musicologist—I think, research, and write about music. And I enjoy it! I love sharing my thoughts about music and listen to others talking about their understanding of the music they listen to. I’m also a musician and perform and teach Persian setar. I have experience in teaching music, and other topics within a variety of contexts. I have also given instruction through workshops and music tutorials to students with diverse backgrounds.
